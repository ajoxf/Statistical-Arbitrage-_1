Strategy Explanation

How the Pairs Trading Strategy Works:
  1. Calculate the Spread: The price difference between EURUSD=X and GBPUSD=X, adjusted by the hedge ratio.
  2. Identify Deviation: When the spread moves more than 2.0 standard deviations from its mean, a trading opportunity exists.
  3. Enter Position: Go long the spread when it's too low (buy EURUSD=X, sell GBPUSD=X), go short when it's too high.
  4. Exit Position: Close the trade when the spread returns to its mean.
